How it works

One account in, controlled cards out: every limit enforced.

Fund a single Root account, issue a card to everyone who spends for you, and set hard limits we enforce the moment a card is tapped.

01
Plant your Root
Link your bank account and have all your issued cards draw from your Root, with your limits. All card activities monitored in one dashboard, always.
02
Their card, your rules
Create a controlled, digital card for any employee, family member, or dependent. Set how much they can spend, and where — in seconds.
03
Transactions & OCR
Instant transactions and real-time payment authorization. Followed up with AI-assisted OCR for receipt-transaction cross-validation.
04
Personalized AI insights
AI-powered reports to identify spending patterns and potential areas for savings. All for you to hit your financial goals.

What's inside

Every control a Root could want. Nothing a Leaf can break.

Vendor pruning
Allow or block any merchant per Leaf, instantly. Gaming off, or groceries on — it's all by your rules.
Spend limits
Set a periodic limit per Leaf. Bonzai stops the card at the line, with no overdraft and no surprises.
Instant cards
Issue a virtual card quickly, add it to Apple or Google Pay, instantly freeze it with one tap.
Receipt OCR
Snap a receipt and Bonzai reads it, capturing line items, vendor, and total, then files it against the transaction.
Requests & approvals
Leaves ask for more funds or a new vendor; Root approves or declines from the home dashboard.
Tap to pay
Apple Pay and Google Pay supported on day one, so a branch card works the moment it is issued.

Questions

Everything, before you ask.

The Root is the head account — the trunk all your money is rooted in. It issues Leaf cards, sets their limits, and approves their requests. Everything goes in and out through here, with one owner typically steering the wheel.
A Leaf is a sub-account under your Root with its own card, for an employee, family member, or dependent.
Bonzai itself is not a bank — banking services are provided by licensed, regulated partners in each market. Funds are held with those institutions, data is encrypted end to end, and your bank credentials are never stored by Bonzai.
