How it works

Root, branch, leaf — one living tree of family money.

Plant a Root, graft a branch for each person, and let every leaf of spend stay in view.

01
Plant your Root
Open a Root account and link your bank in under a minute. This is the trunk all family money grows from.
02
Graft a Branch
Issue a controlled virtual card to a child or family member — set a weekly limit and choose exactly which vendors it can reach.
03
Watch it grow
AI tracks every leaf of spend, flags what needs pruning, and shows one balance line that turns gold, terracotta, or green.

What's inside

Every control a Root could want. Nothing a branch can break.

Vendor pruning
Allow or block any merchant per branch, instantly. Casinos off, groceries on — your rules.
Spend limits
Set a weekly cap per branch. Bonzai stops the card at the line — no overdraft, no surprises.
Instant cards
Issue a virtual card in sixty seconds, add it to Apple or Google Pay, freeze it with one tap.
Receipt OCR
Snap a receipt and Bonzai reads it — line items, vendor, total — and files it against the transaction.
Requests & approvals

Questions

Everything, before you ask.

The Root is the parent account — the trunk all family money grows from. It holds the balance, issues branch cards, sets limits and vendor rules, and approves requests. One adult typically owns the Root.
A Branch is a sub-account under your Root, usually for a child or family member, with its own controlled card. A Leaf is an individual spend — every transaction a branch makes. Bonzai keeps every leaf in view of the Root.
Bonzai itself is not a bank — banking services are provided by licensed, regulated partners in each market. Funds are held with those institutions, data is encrypted end to end, and your bank credentials are never stored by Bonzai.
Pricing for founding families will be shared with your early-access invite. The concept is built around a simple per-Root subscription with unlimited branch cards — no per-card fees and no interest products.
We are onboarding founding families across the GCC through 2026, with Qatar first (balances shown in Qatari Riyal). Other markets open in waves as regulated partners come online.
Bonzai is in concept and early access. Join the waitlist and we will send an invite as your region and a founding cohort open up.
